About Me

Hi, I'm Grant Hough, a senior at Saratoga High School with interests in computer science and percussion. When I'm not at my desk, I'm probably out in the mountains biking or skiing.

My Interests

Machine Learning

Until recently, I've perceived machine learning as magic. After learning how neural networks really work, I've made it a goal to use this technology to solve the world's pressing issues.

App Development

I began my journey into CS with a basic iOS app. I now use app development as a creative outlet for my ideas and machine learning models.

Percussion

Ever since I was young, I've loved to create rhythm and sound. Percussion is the perfect medium for my urge to make noise.

Machine Learning

I've created ML models with both PyTorch and TensorFlow and implemented these models into a number of applications. I have experience using various neural network types and architectures for things like image classification and sentiment detection. These technologies are ones I frequently use in software development competitions such as hackathons. If you're looking to get started with ML, here is a video I created that explains the basics of neural networks.


App Development

I use Swift and SwiftUI to create iOS apps. Having knowledge of mobile app development allows me to take advantage of mobile-specific techonologies, which is vital to projects like Find My Pet. For web applications such as websites and browser extensions, I use HTML, CSS and JavaScript along with libraries such as jQuery. I particularly love browser extensions because of their typically autonomous nature.


Percussion

In the summer of 2022, I marched as a member of the snareline for the Blue Devils 'B' Drum and Bugle Corps. Outside of drum corps, I'm percussion captain for the marching and winter, and concert percussion programs at Saratoga High School. Additionally, I will be participating in the CBDA All-State Concert Band for 2023. I've also taught private lessons to kids in my community.


My Software Projects




  • OK2Say

    OK2Say helps users keep their sentences uncontroversial by using NLP to evaluate user text for controversial sentences. (1st place winner and Zūm Sponsor Prize winner at SchoolHacks)


    band+aid

    band+aid is an iOS app designed to bring world-class music education to middle and high-school band students.


    Find My Pet

    Find My Pet is a mobile application that uses ML to allow citizens to easily find their lost pets. (Best Youth Hack winner at RU Hacks)


  • Corally

    Corally is a web app that uses machine learning to ensure that coral reef organizations always have up-to-date information on the status of coral reefs with the help of users across the world. (Best Social Cause Hack winner at HackBytes)


    Skin Hub

    Skin Hub is an iOS app that utilizes machine learning to identify skin conditions and provide information on them.


    Drive Cleaners

    Drive Cleaners is an iOS app that utilizes underused clothes appliances and allows people who don't have the time or ability to wash their own clothes access to clean laundry. (Created with Neal Malhotra)


  • Forecast

    Forecast is an app that uses ML to predict the stock market (Best On-Theme-Hack winner at The Hackconomist).


    SpeakEasy

    SpeakEasy is a browser extension that uses ML to suggest less offensive rephrased alternatives to user sentences (2nd place, Congressional App Challenge).


    Neural Studio

    Neural Studio is an iOS app created to help users visualize the effects of hyperparameters on neural network performance.
